• CREATIVE → Maximize divergent thinking, seek surprising connections
• DEBUG → Root cause focus, systematic elimination

• What mental model does this reveal or reinforce?

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• What do you now understand that you couldn't before this analysis?

• What would change if you ran this analysis again with fresh eyes?
