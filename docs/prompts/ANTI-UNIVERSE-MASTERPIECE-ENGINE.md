## THE ANTI-UNIVERSE MATRIX

Every idea has **seven dimensions** that can be inverted. Choose 1-3 inversions per idea for coherence:

### The Seven Inversions

| Dimension        | Original         | Inverted                   | Example                                                                                          |
| ---------------- | ---------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **USER**         | Who uses it      | Who NEVER uses such tools  | Pomodoro timer for people who hate time-boxing â†’ Pomodoro for procrastinators (celebrates delay) |
| **CONTEXT**      | When/where used  | Opposite environment       | Meditation app â†’ Meditation for chaotic environments (subways, offices)                          |

### Inversion Combinations That Create Masterpieces
