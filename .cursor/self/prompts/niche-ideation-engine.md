### Step 2: Pain Point Amplification

For each potential niche, probe deeper with these questions:

```
PAIN INTENSITY CHECKLIST
========================

â–¡ FREQUENCY: How often does this pain occur?
  - Daily pain = high retention potential
  - Weekly pain = moderate retention
  - Monthly/yearly = one-time purchase

â–¡ URGENCY: What happens if they don't solve it?
  - Money loss = very high willingness to pay
  - Time waste = high willingness to pay
  - Frustration = moderate willingness to pay
  - Mild inconvenience = low willingness to pay

â–¡ CURRENT SOLUTIONS: What do they use now?
  - Spreadsheets/paper = massive opportunity
  - General-purpose app = moderate opportunity
  - Competitor app with 3+ star reviews = differentiation needed
  - Well-loved specialized tool = avoid

â–¡ SWITCHING COST: How hard to change behavior?
  - They're already doing something manually = easy adoption
  - They use a free tool they hate = easy adoption
  - They use paid software they're invested in = hard adoption

â–¡ COMMUNITY DENSITY: Can you find 10,000 of them online?
  - Active subreddit with 50k+ members = yes
  - Facebook groups with engagement = yes
  - Professional associations = yes
  - Have to explain the niche = no
```

### Step 3: Monetization Viability Filter

Every idea must pass ALL of these gates:

```
MONETIZATION GATES
==================

GATE 1: PRICE TOLERANCE
- Can you charge $10+ without explaining why?
- Is there a professional version at $25+?
- Does the value exceed the price obviously?

GATE 2: ACQUISITION PATH
- Can you name 3 specific places to find customers?
- Is there a community leader who could promote this?
- Does the tool create shareable outputs?

GATE 3: RETENTION HOOK
- Will they open it at least weekly?
- Does data accumulate over time (switching cost)?
- Is there a streak/progress/status element?

GATE 4: COMPETITIVE MOAT
- Is the niche too small for big players?
- Does offline-first create differentiation?
- Is there domain expertise required?

GATE 5: SCOPE CONTROL
- Can MVP be built in 4-6 weeks?
- Are the APIs proven and documented?
- Is the core value in 3 screens or less?
```
