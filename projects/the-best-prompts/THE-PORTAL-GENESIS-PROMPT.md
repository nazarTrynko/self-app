## THE VISION: A PORTAL, NOT PAGES

Forget "5 landing pages." Think: ONE UNIFIED EXPERIENCE that contains multitudes.

The portal has:
1. **A Hub** — The entry point that IS the experience (not just navigation)
2. **Chambers** — Individual prompt suites that feel like entering different worlds
3. **A Unifying Concept** — A metaphor that ties everything together and makes exploration feel like discovery
4. **Magic Interactions** — Things that make people say "how did they do that?"
5. **Shareable Outputs** — Users create things they WANT to post

## THE PHILOSOPHY

**Why prompt sites fail:**
- They're catalogues (boring)
- They look the same (forgettable)
- They don't DO anything (passive)
- They're built for search engines, not humans (soulless)

**Why THIS will win:**
- It's an EXPERIENCE you enter
- Each section has its own world
- You create things you're proud of
- You discover things you didn't know you needed
- You WANT to share what you found

## THE METAPHOR CANDIDATES

Choose ONE unifying concept for the portal. Consider:

**The Library of Selves** — An infinite library where each chamber contains prompts for a different version of you (the writer, the strategist, the healer, the builder)

**The Forge** — A workshop where raw intentions become refined outputs. Different stations for different crafts.

**The Observatory** — Looking inward through different lenses. Each telescope reveals a different truth.

**The Garden** — Seeds (prompts) you plant grow into different things. Seasonal, cyclical, nurturing.

**The Laboratory** — Experiments in thought. Beakers, formulas, controlled reactions that produce insight.

**The Constellation Map** — Navigate by stars. Each constellation is a domain. Zoom in to individual stars (prompts).

Or invent something better. The metaphor must:
- Enable natural navigation
- Make exploration feel like discovery
- Create emotional resonance
- Be visually generative (suggests imagery, colors, shapes)

## THE HUB REQUIREMENTS

The hub is NOT a boring homepage. It's the portal entrance.

**Must Have:**
1. **Immediate "Wow"** — Within 3 seconds, users know this is different
2. **Interactive Discovery** — You don't read a menu, you EXPLORE
3. **Visual Navigation** — Not just links, but a visual map/interface
4. **Animated Presence** — It feels alive, not static
5. **Sound Optional** — Subtle audio that enhances (can be muted)
6. **Mobile Magic** — Touch interactions that feel native and delightful

**The Hub Should:**
- Show all "chambers" (prompt suites) in a visual interface
- Allow exploration without clicking away
- Preview what each chamber contains
- Have its own atmosphere (not just navigation)
- Make users WANT to explore every chamber

**Design Possibilities:**
- 3D space you navigate
- Constellation map you zoom into
- Cards that expand and reveal
- Scrolling panorama with depth
- Interactive canvas with floating elements
- Morphing shapes that represent domains

## THE CHAMBERS (5 Required)

Each chamber is a complete world. NOT just a page with prompts.

### Chamber Requirements:
1. **Distinct Atmosphere** — Walking into each chamber feels like entering a new space
2. **Unique Color World** — Each has its own palette (not just accent color swap)
3. **Signature Interaction** — Each has ONE interaction that's unique to it
4. **Complete Prompt Suite** — 4-7 prompts in sequence
5. **Tool Where Appropriate** — 3 of 5 must have client-side interactive tools
6. **Output Creation** — Users make something they can export/share

### The 5 Chambers:

**Chamber 1: DECISIONS (Interactive Tool)**
- Domain: Making hard choices with clarity
- Tool: Interactive decision matrix that scores and ranks options
- Atmosphere: Industrial, forge-like, molten metal and precision
- Signature: Weighing animation as options are compared

**Chamber 2: CONTENT (Interactive Tool)**
- Domain: Creating hooks and content that captures attention
- Tool: Hook formula engine that generates scroll-stoppers
- Atmosphere: Electric, neon, attention-economy aesthetic
- Signature: "Viral potential" meter that reacts to generated hooks

**Chamber 3: REFLECTION (Interactive Tool)**
- Domain: Annual review, life audit, self-assessment
- Tool: Interactive life audit workbook with domain scoring
- Atmosphere: Contemplative, seasonal, golden-hour warmth
- Signature: Radar chart that visualizes life balance

**Chamber 4: HEALING (Pure Suite)**
- Domain: Therapeutic journaling, inner dialogue, self-compassion
- Tool: None (prompts ARE the tool, sensitivity requires simplicity)
- Atmosphere: Soft, water-like, breathing rhythms
- Signature: Text that appears slowly, respectfully

**Chamber 5: CREATION (Pure Suite)**
- Domain: Fiction writing, story seeds, creative generation
- Tool: None (prompts are generative enough)
- Atmosphere: Literary, ink and parchment, starlit
- Signature: Prompts that "write themselves" with typewriter effect

## DESIGN CONSTRAINTS (Non-Negotiable)

**Typography:**
- NO Inter, Roboto, Arial, system fonts
- Each chamber gets its OWN font pairing
- Hub gets a distinctive display font
- Readable at all sizes

**Color:**
- NO purple gradients (overdone)
- NO startup-blue (#007bff variants)
- Each chamber has 4-5 color palette
- Hub has its own unifying palette that HINTS at all chambers

**Layout:**
- NO generic card grids
- Embrace asymmetry
- Use negative space dramatically
- Create depth (layers, shadows, overlaps)

**Motion:**
- Meaningful animation (not decoration)
- Performance-first (CSS over JS where possible)
- Respect reduced-motion preferences
- Create rhythm and breathing

**Technical:**
- Pure HTML/CSS/JS (no frameworks)
- < 3s load time
- Mobile-first, touch-optimized
- Works offline for tools (localStorage)
- Accessible (keyboard nav, screen readers)
