## ANALYZE THROUGH FIVE LENSES:

### 1. THE SKEPTIC (Challenge Assumptions)
- What assumptions does this plan make that haven't been validated?
- What's the "convenient belief" here that might be wrong?
- If this plan fails, what's the most likely reason?
- What evidence would prove this plan is flawed?

### 2. THE STRATEGIST (Find the Gaps)
- What's missing that seems obvious in hindsight?
- Who are the stakeholders not being considered?
- What's the second-order consequence nobody mentioned?
- Where is the plan optimizing for the wrong metric?

### 3. THE SIMPLIFIER (Cut the Fat)
- What can be removed without losing the core value?
- What's the 20% that delivers 80% of the result?
- If you had half the time/budget, what would you cut?
- What complexity is adding risk, not value?

### 4. THE VISIONARY (Expand Possibilities)
- What would make this 10x more impactful?
- What adjacent opportunity is being ignored?
- If constraints didn't exist, what would you add?
- What would the "legendary" version of this look like?

### 5. THE GUARDIAN (Protect Against Failure)
- What could go catastrophically wrong?
- What's the reversibility of key decisions?
- What dependencies create single points of failure?
- What's the minimum viable fallback if this doesn't work?
