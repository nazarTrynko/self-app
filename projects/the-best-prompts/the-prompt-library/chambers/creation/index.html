II

The Strange Door

Excavates: Unexpected premises 
I want premises that feel both impossible and inevitable.

Give me 7 "What if" scenarios that break reality in interesting ways:

For each:
1. **The What If:** State the premise in one sentence
2. **The World:** How does this change everything?
3. **The Character:** Who would be most affected/transformed by this reality?
4. **The Conflict:** What becomes difficult that wasn't before?
5. **The Resonance:** What human truth does this fantasy explore?

I want premises that are:
- Specific (not "what if magic existed")
- Emotional (not just clever)
- Explorable (could sustain a novel)
- Strange but not absurd

Avoid: Time travel, zombies, chosen ones, love triangles, dystopian governments. Give me something I haven't seen.
You'll get: 7 original premises with depth
