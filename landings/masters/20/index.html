NOW

Every moment you spend reading this is a moment you could be at a threshold. The door is always there. The question is whether you'll walk through it.

Why We Miss Thresholds

We're designed for Chronos—the steady tick of sequential time. Kairos requires a different kind of attention. The crossroads doesn't announce itself. It waits to be recognized.

The Inversion

Every tool focuses on analyzing the past or planning the future. The Threshold focuses on the present—the only moment where choice is actually possible. Past and future are stories. Now is the only real thing.

The Practice

Each day, ask: "Am I at a threshold right now?" Not every moment is a crossroads. But more are than you realize. The skill is learning to recognize when you're standing at a door.

Close your eyes. Feel this moment. Not the past moment, not the next one. This one. Is something asking to change? Is there a door? You may be at a threshold right now and not know it.
