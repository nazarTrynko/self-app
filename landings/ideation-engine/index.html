The 5 FiltersMust Pass ALL

Every idea must survive all five filters. Fail one, and you KILL or SIMPLIFY. No exceptions.

PAIN

—

Who hurts and how badly?

KILL: Vague pain
PASS: Specific, acute pain expressed in user's own words

DISTRIBUTION

—

Where are they already?

KILL: No channel
PASS: Clear channel where target users already gather

EMOTION

KILL: Weak feeling
