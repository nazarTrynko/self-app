The Concept

Time is Space

CHRONO reimagines time management by transforming the abstract concept of time into a navigable 3D space. Instead of viewing your calendar as a flat grid, you literally walk through your day as if moving through rooms in a building.

Each hour is a corridor. Each meeting is a room you enter. Each task is an object you interact with. Our brains evolved to navigate physical space, not abstract time. By mapping time to space, CHRONO leverages millions of years of spatial reasoning.
