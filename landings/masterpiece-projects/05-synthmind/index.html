The Concept

Visualize Collective Thought

SYNTHMIND is a collaborative thinking tool where participants' ideas appear as nodes in a shared neural network visualization. As you add thoughts, they connect to related ideas from others, forming visible synaptic connections.

The more people engage with a node, the larger and more connected it becomes. Watch ideas evolve, merge, branch, and form clusters in real-time‚Äîa literal visualization of collective intelligence.
