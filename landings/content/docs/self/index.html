Philosophy

The Way of the Prompt

The quality of your thinking is determined by the quality of your questions.

I

Specificity Creates Clarity

Vague inputs force guessing. Specific inputs enable precise reasoning.

‚ùå "Help me with my code"
‚úÖ "I'm building a rate limiter for an API handling 1000 req/s..."
II

Uncertainty Deepens Engagement

Admitting what you don't know engages Oracle and Critic minds.

‚ùå "Implement JWT authentication"
‚úÖ "I'm torn between JWT and session-based auth..."
III

Stakes Calibrate Confidence

High stakes activate Guardian. SELF will warn, validate, and protect.

‚ùå "Add a delete button"
‚úÖ "Add delete for user accounts. Production code, real data..."
IV

Multi-Ask Activates Multi-Mind

Each question activates a different mind. More questions = richer blend.

‚ùå "What should I do?"
‚úÖ "What should I do? What could go wrong? What am I missing?"
V

Forward Questions Trigger Prediction

Asking about the future activates the prediction engine.

‚ùå "How do I implement this?"
‚úÖ "How do I implement this? What will I need to think about after?"
