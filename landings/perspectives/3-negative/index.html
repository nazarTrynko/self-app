Move to reveal what hides in plain sight

NEGATIVE SPACE

What defines a window—

the glass, or the view it frames?

What defines a word—

the letters, or the silence around them?

What defines you—

what you are, or what you choose not to be?

"The usefulness of a pot comes from its emptiness."
— Lao Tzu

Every decision is also

the death of its alternatives.

Every word spoken

silences a thousand others.

The most important things

are often what isn't said.
