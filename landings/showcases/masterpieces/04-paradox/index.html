Why Paradox?

The deepest truths are paradoxical. Light is both wave and particle. You are both the observer and the observed. The universe began from nothing and contains everything. Your binary mind was not built for this—but it can learn.

The Koan Tradition

Zen masters gave students koans—paradoxes impossible to solve logically. "What is the sound of one hand clapping?" The point isn't to answer. The point is to break the mind that demands answers.

The Inversion

Every other tool helps you resolve contradictions. This one asks you to hold them. To sit in the discomfort of both/and. To discover that reality is larger than your logic.

Close your eyes. Hold both sides of the contradiction in your mind simultaneously. Don't try to resolve. Don't choose. Just... hold. Notice what happens when the mind stops demanding resolution.
