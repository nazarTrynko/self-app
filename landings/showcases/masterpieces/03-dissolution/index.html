The Art of Subtraction

Sculptors remove stone to reveal the form within. Strategic forgetting removes mental weight to reveal the self beneath. You become by unbecoming.

The Inversion

Every other tool adds to your cognitive load. The Dissolution is the opposite—controlled entropy. Sometimes the path forward requires traveling lighter.

Not Suppression

This isn't about pushing things down. It's about genuinely releasing what no longer serves you. The difference is intention—and ceremony.

Close your eyes. Imagine the belief or pattern as a physical weight you carry. Feel its heaviness. Now, imagine setting it down. Watch it dissolve into particles of light. Feel the space it leaves behind.
