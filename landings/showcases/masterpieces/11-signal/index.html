Common Avoidance Signatures

◇

Financial Content

Avoiding money topics often signals fear of inadequacy, judgment about worth, or unprocessed shame about decisions.

◇

Health Information

Skipping health content may indicate mortality anxiety, body shame, or resistance to lifestyle changes you know you need.

◇

Relationship Messages

Avoiding certain people's messages reveals unresolved tension, guilt, or truths you're not ready to address.

◇

Career/Success Content

Scrolling past achievement content may signal comparison wounds, imposter fears, or unlived ambitions.

The Negative Space

In art, negative space defines the positive. In attention, what you avoid defines what matters. Your avoidance pattern is a map of your psyche's defended territories.

The Inversion

Every other tool tracks engagement—clicks, likes, time spent. The Signal tracks disengagement. Because the things you refuse to look at are the things that need your attention most.

The Practice

For one week, notice not what you consume, but what you avoid. The flinch. The scroll-past. The "I'll deal with that later." That's where your growth edge lives.

Right now, there's something you're avoiding thinking about. Not the thing that came to mind first—that's the decoy. The real avoidance is beneath that. What is it? The resistance you feel reading this is itself a signal.
