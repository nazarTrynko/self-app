# Magnifier Pro

**ID:** 088
**Category:** Accessibility
**Tier:** Premium ($8)
**APIs:** Camera, Flashlight, Image Processing
**Offline:** Full

---

## One-Liner

A powerful magnifying glass app with stabilization, contrast enhancement, and freeze-frame for people with low vision.

## Problem

Built-in magnifiers are basic. People with low vision need more: image stabilization, high-contrast modes, ability to freeze and examine images, and customizable color filters.

## Solution

Advanced magnification with features specifically designed for accessibility: shake stabilization, multiple contrast modes, freeze-frame with zoom, and flashlight integration.

## Target User

- People with low vision or macular degeneration
- Seniors reading small print
- Anyone needing occasional magnification

## Key Features

- 16x optical + digital zoom
- Image stabilization
- High-contrast modes (yellow/black, white/black)
- Freeze frame for steady examination
- Integrated flashlight
- Quick-access widget

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $7.99
**Strategy:** Low vision communities, senior centers, optometrist referrals
