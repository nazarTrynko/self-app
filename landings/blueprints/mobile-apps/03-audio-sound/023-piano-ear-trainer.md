# Ear Training Pro

**ID:** 023
**Category:** Audio & Sound
**Tier:** Pro ($25)
**APIs:** Web Audio API, Microphone, File System
**Offline:** Full

---

## One-Liner

Comprehensive ear training for musicians with interval recognition, chord identification, rhythm dictation, and progress tracking.

## Problem

Developing musical ear takes consistent practice, but lessons are expensive and apps are either too basic or require subscriptions. Musicians need structured, progressive training they can do anywhere without internet.

## Solution

A complete ear training curriculum covering intervals, chords, scales, rhythm, and melody dictation with adaptive difficulty, detailed progress analytics, and certification-track exercisesâ€”all working offline.

## Target User

- Music students preparing for exams
- Self-taught musicians developing skills
- Vocalists training relative pitch
- Music teachers assigning homework
- Audio engineers developing critical listening

## Key Features

- Interval recognition (all intervals, all directions)
- Chord quality identification (triads through extended)
- Scale mode identification
- Rhythm dictation exercises
- Melodic dictation
- Adaptive difficulty algorithm
- Detailed progress statistics
- ABRSM/RCM grade-aligned exercises

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $24.99
**Strategy:** Music education forums, conservatory partnerships, music teacher networks, YouTube music education channels

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- Web Audio API: Tone/chord synthesis, playback
- Microphone: Optional singing input for pitch matching
- File System: Progress data storage

**Offline Strategy:**
All sounds synthesized. Exercise database embedded. Progress stored locally.

**Data Handling:**
All progress local. Optional export for teachers/students to share.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** EarMaster (desktop, expensive), Functional Ear Trainer (limited), Teoria (web-based)
**Our Edge:** Mobile + offline, comprehensive curriculum, adaptive difficulty, progress analytics, one-time purchase

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Medium-High
**Timeline:** 8-10 weeks
**Key Challenges:** High-quality instrument synthesis, adaptive difficulty algorithm, curriculum design
