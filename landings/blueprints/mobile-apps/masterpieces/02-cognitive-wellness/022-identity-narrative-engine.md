## One-Liner

A personal mythology system that helps you author, strengthen, and evolve your life narrativeâ€”integrating experiences into meaningful stories that build resilience, purpose, and a coherent sense of identity.

## Problem

Identity is constructed through narrative. Without intentional story-making, life experiences remain fragmented, setbacks feel meaningless, and identity becomes reactive rather than authored. Narrative therapy and life review interventions have powerful effects, but are typically only accessible through expensive therapy. Most people never integrate their experiences into coherent, empowering life stories.

## Solution

A guided life narrative construction system that helps you identify core themes, integrate difficult experiences, recognize growth patterns, articulate values and purpose, and continuously author a life story that provides meaning, resilience, and direction.

## Target User

- People in life transitions seeking meaning (midlife, retirement, divorce)
- Trauma survivors integrating experiences
- Recent graduates constructing adult identity
- Professionals seeking clarity on purpose and direction
- Therapists wanting tools for client narrative work
- Writers working on memoir or personal essays
- Anyone feeling their life lacks coherent meaning
- People approaching end of life wanting legacy

## Key Features

- **Life Chapter Structure**: Organize life into meaningful chapters and arcs
- **Theme Discovery**: AI-assisted identification of recurring life themes
- **Experience Integration**: Guided protocols for meaning-making from events
- **Redemption Narratives**: Transform setbacks into growth stories
- **Value Archaeology**: Discover values through life pattern analysis
- **Future Authoring**: Write forward-looking narrative (Peterson-inspired)
- **Photo-Memory Integration**: Connect photos to narrative moments
- **Turning Point Mapping**: Identify and articulate pivotal moments
- **Generativity Prompts**: Create legacy statements and wisdom distillation
- **Narrative Coherence Score**: Assess and improve story integration
- **Export to Memoir**: Generate prose from structured narrative
- **Family Story Connection**: Link personal narrative to ancestral context

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- Speech Framework: Voice-based narrative entry
- NaturalLanguage: Theme extraction and sentiment analysis
- Core ML: Pattern recognition across life stories
- Photos: Timeline integration with narrative
- Calendar: Event correlation with life chapters

**Offline Strategy:**
All narrative processing on-device. Life story database encrypted locally. Theme analysis runs locally. No cloud dependency.

**Data Handling:**
- Life narratives: Maximum encryption, device-only
- Photos referenced: Not copied, only linked
- Analysis: All local ML processing
- Export: User-controlled formats
- This is the most private data imaginable

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:**
- Journaling apps (daily focus, no life-span view)
- Memoir software (writing tools, no meaning-making)
- Therapy apps (symptom focus, not narrative)
- Life review tools (legacy focus, not active authoring)

**Our Edge:**
- Active life authoring, not passive recording
- AI-assisted theme and pattern discovery
- Integration protocols for difficult experiences
- Future authoring for direction-setting
- Narrative therapy principles made accessible
- Coherence scoring and improvement
