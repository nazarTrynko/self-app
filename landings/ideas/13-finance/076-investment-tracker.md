- DIY portfolio managers

## Key Features

- Manual position entry
- Stock, ETF, crypto, fund support
- Offline price updates (manual refresh)
- Cost basis and gain tracking
- Dividend tracking
- Asset allocation visualization
- Performance vs benchmarks
