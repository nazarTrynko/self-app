## One-Liner

Fast incident logging that produces parent/admin-ready summaries—offline.

## Problem

Teachers spend hours documenting after the fact; details get lost and reports feel inconsistent.

## Solution

One-tap logging with tags and templates that auto-generates clear summaries and timelines.

## Target User

Teachers, special education staff, administrators.

## Key Features

- Ultra-fast incident logging templates
- Tag system (behavior, trigger, response, outcome)
- Student profiles (minimal fields)
- Summary generator (weekly/monthly PDF)
- Privacy-first local store + export controls
