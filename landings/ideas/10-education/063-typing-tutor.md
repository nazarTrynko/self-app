## One-Liner

Learn touch typing on mobile with adaptive lessonsâ€”build keyboard proficiency for phones and tablets.

## Problem

Mobile typing skills are increasingly important but undertaught. Existing typing tutors focus on desktop keyboards. People hunt-and-peck on phones, limiting their productivity.

## Solution

A mobile-first typing tutor that teaches efficient thumb typing techniques, tracks speed and accuracy, and adapts lessons to problem keysâ€”becoming proficient on your phone's keyboard.

## Target User

- Young people learning to type
- Professionals wanting faster mobile typing
- Students taking notes on tablets
- Anyone frustrated with slow phone typing
- ESL learners with unfamiliar keyboards

## Key Features

- Mobile keyboard focused lessons
- Adaptive practice on weak keys
- WPM and accuracy tracking
- Multiple keyboard layouts
- Custom text practice (paste your own)
- Progress over time charts
- Daily practice goals
- Swipe typing training option

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $3.99
**Strategy:** Productivity communities, student forums, professional development, ESL learning groups

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- File System: Progress storage
- Keyboard Events: Typing capture

**Offline Strategy:**
All lessons and progress local. No network required.

**Data Handling:**
Typing statistics stored locally. No personal content stored beyond progress.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Typing.com (web), TypingClub (web), Keybr (desktop focused)
**Our Edge:** Mobile-first design, offline, thumb typing focus, adaptive practice

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Low-Medium
**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Accurate key tracking on mobile keyboards, adaptive lesson generation, engaging practice content
