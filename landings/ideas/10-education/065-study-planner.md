## One-Liner

Academic planner with assignment tracking, exam countdown, study scheduling, and GPA calculation for students.

## Problem

Students juggle multiple classes, assignments, and exams with inadequate tools. Generic to-do apps don't understand academic workflows. Existing student planners require subscriptions or internet.

## Solution

A purpose-built academic planner that organizes by semester and course, tracks assignments with grades, schedules study time, and projects GPA impactâ€”designed for how students actually work.

## Target User

- High school students
- University undergraduates
- Graduate students
- Professional certification students
- Anyone in structured education

## Key Features

- Semester and course organization
- Assignment tracking with due dates
- Grade tracking and GPA calculation
- Exam schedule with countdowns
- Study session planning
- Syllabus document storage
- Grade goal projections
- Academic calendar view

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $17.99
**Strategy:** Student forums, university subreddits, back-to-school marketing, academic success blogs

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- File System: Course and assignment data
- Notifications: Due date reminders
- Calendar API: Schedule integration

**Offline Strategy:**
All academic data stored locally. Reminders scheduled locally. No cloud required.

**Data Handling:**
Academic records stored locally. Export available for records.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** MyStudyLife (cloud-required), iStudiez (aging), generic task apps
**Our Edge:** GPA projection, academic-specific features, one-time purchase, offline-first

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Medium
**Timeline:** 6-8 weeks
**Key Challenges:** GPA calculation flexibility (different systems), schedule complexity, intuitive academic UX
