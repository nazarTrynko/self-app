## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- File System: Meeting data storage
- Calendar API: Meeting import
- Notifications: Prep and follow-up reminders

**Offline Strategy:**
All meeting data local. Calendar sync is one-way import. No cloud dependency.

**Data Handling:**
Participant notes and meeting content stored locally. Full privacy for sensitive meeting content.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Meeting note apps (post-meeting only), calendar apps (no preparation), CRM (enterprise, expensive)
**Our Edge:** Full meeting lifecycle, participant history, preparation focused, offline, one-time purchase

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Medium
**Timeline:** 5-6 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Meeting template flexibility, action item extraction UX, calendar integration
