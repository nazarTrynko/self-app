# Journey Log

**ID:** 081
**Category:** Travel
**Tier:** Premium ($10)
**APIs:** Camera, GPS, File System
**Offline:** Full

---

## One-Liner

Beautiful travel journal with automatic location tracking, photo organization, and map visualization of your adventures.

## Problem

Travel photos end up in camera roll chaos. Memories of where you went and when fade. Travel journaling apps require internet. No offline solution creates beautiful trip memories.

## Solution

A travel journaling app that organizes photos by trip and location, creates beautiful timelines, and plots your journey on mapsâ€”all working offline during your travels.

## Target User

- Travel enthusiasts documenting adventures
- Family vacationers creating memories
- Digital nomads tracking journeys
- Solo travelers journaling experiences
- Anyone wanting organized travel memories

## Key Features

- Trip creation with date ranges
- Auto-organize photos by location
- Map visualization of journey
- Daily journal entries
- Offline map support
- Statistics (countries, cities, distance)
- Export as photo book PDF
- Memory timeline view

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $9.99
**Strategy:** Travel blogger communities, photography forums, digital nomad groups, travel planning communities

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- Camera: Photo capture with metadata
- Geolocation: Location tagging
- File System: Trip and photo storage

**Offline Strategy:**
Maps pre-downloaded per trip. Photos and entries stored locally. Full offline operation.

**Data Handling:**
All travel data local. Export creates shareable formats. No cloud sync.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Day One (general journaling), Google Photos (auto-organize but cloud), Polarsteps (requires internet)
**Our Edge:** Travel-focused, offline maps, journey visualization, one-time purchase

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Medium-High
**Timeline:** 8-10 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Offline map implementation, photo organization by location, journey visualization
