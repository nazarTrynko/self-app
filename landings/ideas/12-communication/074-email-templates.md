## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- Clipboard API: Quick template copy
- Share API: Direct to email apps
- File System: Template storage

**Offline Strategy:**
All templates stored locally. No cloud sync. Works without internet.

**Data Handling:**
Templates and usage data stored locally. Variable values not persisted.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Gmail canned responses (limited), TextExpander (subscription), email client features
**Our Edge:** Standalone, works with any email, professional templates included, one-time purchase

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Low-Medium
**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Variable replacement UX, integration with various email apps, template quality
