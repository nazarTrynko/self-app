## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- File System: Puzzle data, progress
- Notifications: Daily puzzle reminder
- Canvas API: Puzzle rendering

**Offline Strategy:**
Puzzles pre-generated and bundled. Daily puzzles determined by date algorithm. All local.

**Data Handling:**
Progress stored locally. No personal data collected.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Individual puzzle apps (ad-heavy), NYT Games (subscription), various free apps
**Our Edge:** Multiple puzzle types, no ads, one-time purchase, clean design, offline

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Medium
**Timeline:** 5-6 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Puzzle generation algorithms, multiple puzzle type implementations, engaging daily mechanic
