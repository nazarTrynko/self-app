## One-Liner

Prebuilt call scripts and one-tap phrases for anxiety/stutter support—offline.

## Problem

People freeze on calls and avoid necessary conversations (appointments, utilities, work) due to anxiety, stutter, or cognitive load.

## Solution

Choose a call scenario, follow a script, and use one-tap phrases or TTS playback to communicate confidently.

## Target User

People with speech anxiety, stutterers, neurodivergent users, caregivers.

## Key Features

- Scenario scripts (doctor, landlord, HR, customer support)
- One-tap phraseboard (customizable)
- Optional TTS speak-out mode
- Call prep checklist + notes
- Post-call summary template
