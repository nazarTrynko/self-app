## One-Liner

Guided walkthroughs that create claim-ready “before” baselines with structured photo proof.

## Problem

Insurance disputes and landlord conflicts often hinge on “pre-existing damage,” but most people lack organized baseline proof.

## Solution

A guided room-by-room capture flow that produces a timestamped baseline packet you can export anytime.

## Target User

Homeowners, renters, landlords, property managers.

## Key Features

- Room-by-room guided checklist (photos required)
- Damage tagging + severity + notes
- Timeline view for updates over time
- Export baseline packet (PDF + attachments)
- Reminders for seasonal updates
