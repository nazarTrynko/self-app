---

## One-Liner

Build professional agreements from clause blocks—offline, fast, and consistent.

## Problem

Freelancers use risky templates and miss critical clauses, creating disputes and unpaid work.

## Solution

Choose a contract type, assemble clauses via a guided builder, then sign and export a clean PDF.

## Target User

Freelancers, consultants, small agencies.

## Key Features

- Contract types (SOW, retainer, NDA, change order)
- Clause library with plain-language explanations
- Smart fields (names, dates, scope, deliverables)
- Signature capture + version history
- Export and archive (PDF + editable copy)

## Monetization
