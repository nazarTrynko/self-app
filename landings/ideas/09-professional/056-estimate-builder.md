## One-Liner

Professional estimates and quotes for contractorsâ€”calculate costs, generate proposals, win more jobs on-site.

## Problem

Contractors lose jobs because they can't provide quotes on-site. Estimating software is complex and expensive. Handwritten quotes look unprofessional and lack accuracy.

## Solution

A contractor-focused estimating tool with material/labor databases, markup calculations, and professional proposal generationâ€”create winning quotes during the initial client meeting.

## Target User

- General contractors
- Specialty trade contractors (plumbing, electrical, etc.)
- Remodeling professionals
- Landscapers
- Handymen and service providers

## Key Features

- Item/service price database
- Material quantity calculators
- Labor hour estimation
- Markup and profit margins
- Professional proposal templates
- Optional/alternative items
- Digital signature capture
- Estimate to invoice conversion

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase with trade packs
**Price:** $44.99 base, $9.99 per trade-specific database (plumbing, electrical, etc.)
**Strategy:** Contractor associations, trade schools, home improvement forums, construction communities

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- File System: Estimate and price database storage
- PDF Generation: Professional proposals
- Contacts: Client management
- Camera: Site photo documentation

**Offline Strategy:**
Price databases stored locally. All calculations local. PDF generation on-device.

**Data Handling:**
Business data stored securely locally. Client information protected. Export only through user action.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Jobber (subscription), Houzz Pro (subscription), spreadsheets (unprofessional)
**Our Edge:** One-time purchase, trade-specific databases, true offline, on-site capability

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Medium-High
**Timeline:** 8-10 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Comprehensive item databases, accurate labor estimating, professional PDF output
