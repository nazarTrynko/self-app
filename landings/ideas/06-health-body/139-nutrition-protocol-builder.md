## One-Liner

Build diet protocols with checklists, symptom tracking, and printable plans—offline.

## Problem

People fail diet experiments because there’s no structure, tracking, or clear summary for what worked.

## Solution

Pick a protocol template, track adherence and symptoms, then export a clean “results report.”

## Target User

People managing sensitivities, athletes, biohackers, health experimenters.

## Key Features

- Protocol templates (elimination, macros, timing)
- Daily adherence checklist + notes
- Symptom tracking + simple correlations
- Printable meal-plan outline + shopping prompts
- Export results report (PDF)

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time
**Price:** $14.99
**Strategy:** Sell protocol packs (low FODMAP, athlete cut/bulk) as add-ons.
