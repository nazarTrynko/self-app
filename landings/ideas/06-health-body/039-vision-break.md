# Eye Care Pro

**ID:** 039
**Category:** Health & Body
**Tier:** Micro ($4)
**APIs:** Notifications, Camera (ambient light), File System
**Offline:** Full

---

## One-Liner

Protect your eyes with 20-20-20 reminders, screen time awareness, and vision exercises for digital wellness.

## Problem

Extended screen time causes eye strain, headaches, and vision problems. People forget to take breaks. No solution combines break reminders with actual eye exercises and environmental awareness.

## Solution

A comprehensive eye care companion with smart break reminders, guided eye exercises, ambient light monitoring, and screen time tracking to protect vision during long digital sessions.

## Target User

- Programmers and digital workers
- Students during exam periods
- Gamers with extended sessions
- Anyone experiencing eye strain
- Remote workers

## Key Features

- 20-20-20 rule reminders (customizable)
- Guided eye exercises (focus, movement)
- Ambient light sensor check
- Daily screen time awareness
- Blue light reminder integration
- Eye strain symptom tracker
- Blink reminder during focus
- Weekly eye health reports

## Monetization

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $3.99
**Strategy:** Developer communities, gamer forums, productivity subreddits, digital wellness blogs

## Technical Notes

**Primary APIs:**
- Notifications: Break reminders
- Camera (ambient light sensor): Light level monitoring
- File System: Usage tracking
- Haptics: Gentle reminder vibrations

**Offline Strategy:**
All reminders and exercises local. No network dependency. Data stored locally.

**Data Handling:**
Screen time estimates stored locally. No personal data beyond usage patterns.

## Competition & Differentiation

**Existing Solutions:** Basic break reminder apps, f.lux (just blue light), Time Out (desktop only)
**Our Edge:** Comprehensive (breaks + exercises + light + tracking), mobile-first, one-time purchase

## Development Estimate

**Complexity:** Low
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks
**Key Challenges:** Smart reminder timing, engaging eye exercise UX, ambient light interpretation
